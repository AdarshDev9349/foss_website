Introduction to Open Source

Programme Name: Introduction to Open Source

Date of the event: October 18, 2024

Speaker: Ms. Meharuniza (Research Associate at ICFOSS)

Venue: Seminar Hall, UCEK

Time: 10:00 AM to 12:30 PM

Event Summary

On October 18, 2024, the FOSS Club of the University College of Engineering, Karyavattom (UCEK), organized an engaging session titled "Introduction to Open Source" in the college Seminar Hall. The session was led by Ms. Meharuniza, a Research Associate at ICFOSS (International Centre for Free and Open Source Software). The purpose of the session was to introduce students to the fundamentals of Open Source Software (OSS), its opportunities, and its impact on the global tech community.

Event Highlights

The event began with a welcome address by Ms. Isha Jagath, Chairperson of the FOSS Club, UCEK. Ms. Meharuniza then commenced her session with a clear and concise explanation of what Open Source Software entails. She covered the following key points:

  • The philosophy and principles of open source
  • The four essential freedoms that define Free and Open Source Software
  • How these freedoms empower developers and users to innovate and collaborate
  • Opportunities available in the Open Source domain
  • How contributing to Open Source projects can enhance a developer's skills and career prospects

Interactive Session

The session was highly interactive, with students actively engaging with the speaker. They asked insightful questions about:

  • Starting their journey in Open Source
  • Best practices for contributing to projects
  • How Open Source could be leveraged for career advancement

Ms. Meharuniza addressed their queries thoughtfully, sharing practical tips and resources to help them navigate the Open Source ecosystem.

Conclusion and Impact

The event concluded with a feedback session where students expressed their gratitude and enthusiasm for the session. Ms. Indu Shalini, Head of the Electronics and Communication Department, delivered the Vote of Thanks, praising Ms. Meharuniza for her insightful presentation.

The session was a resounding success, leaving students inspired to explore the vast potential of Open Source Software. It reinforced the FOSS Club's commitment to fostering a culture of learning, collaboration, and innovation among the students of UCEK.
