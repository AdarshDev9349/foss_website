About Us

We are a dedicated team committed to delivering high-quality solutions to our clients. Our mission is to innovate and lead in our industry. FOSS means Free and Open Source Software. It doesn't mean software is free of cost. It means that source code of the software is open for all and anyone is free to use, study and modify the code. This principle allows other people to contribute to the development and improvement of a software like a community.
