Error 418

Programme Name: Error 418 Hackathon

Date of the event: 7th and 8th September 2024

Judges:
Subin Abid (Co-founder and Chief Product Officer at Alpha Geek),
Mehruniza Nazeem (Research Associate at ICFOSS),
Suryan S (Representative from Carestack),
Shaheen Hyder (Founder and CEO of Pygrammers)

Venue: FAYA, Technopark Phase 1, Thejaswini Building

Event Summary

The Error 418 hackathon, held on September 7-8, 2024, at FAYA, Technopark Phase 1, Thejaswini Building, was a resounding success.
This 24-hour event brought together innovative minds to tackle pressing challenges and showcase their coding prowess. The hackathon was completely free, and participants enjoyed complimentary food throughout the event. Ten teams participated in the hackathon, demonstrating exceptional skill and creativity. The event witnessed intense competition, with teams working tirelessly to develop innovative projects.

Event Highlights

The event highlights included a 24-hour hacking marathon, with mentors providing guidance and support throughout.
Our esteemed judges, consisting of industry experts, evaluated projects based on creativity, technicality, and impact.

Our judges were:- Subin Abid (Co-founder and Chief Product Officer at Alpha Geek), Mehruniza Nazeem (Research Associate at ICFOSS), Suryan S (Representative from Carestack), Shaheen Hyder (Founder and CEO of Pygrammers)

Teams tackled various challenges across different domains.The hackathon provided an ideal platform for participants to transform their ideas into reality. As one of the winners noted, "Error 418 provided an ideal platform to transform ideas into reality."

We were delighted to have faculty members from our college visit the event, demonstrating their support for innovation and entrepreneurship.Sabeena Miss and Vidhya Miss interacted with the teams, offered valuable insights, and encouraged them to continue pursuing innovative ideas.Their presence motivated the participants and added a new dimension to the event. The success of Error 418 would not have been possible without the dedication and hard work of the organizing committee.We thank each member for their tireless efforts.

The winners of the Error 418 hackathon were Team StudioOne, consisting of Antony Jaisom (Lead), Vishwanatha Kartha, V Ashiq Rahman, Manuel Dominy, Jenil Biju who impressed the judges with their innovative solution, showcasing exceptional skill and creativity.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Error 418 hackathon demonstrated the potential of collaborative innovation, fostering a community that encourages creativity, technical excellence, and meaningful solutions. We look forward to future editions of this exciting event.
