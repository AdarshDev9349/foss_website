Intro to LinkedIn

Programme Name: Intro to LinkedIn

Date of the event: October 7th

Venue: Online (Google Meet)

Time: 8:00 PM

Event Summary

On October 7th at 8 PM, FOSS-UCEK in collaboration with MULEARN-UCEK organized a special online program, introducing newcomers to the professional networking platform LinkedIn. The program was hosted via Google Meet and aimed to guide beginners through the essentials of LinkedIn, covering everything from creating an optimized profile to understanding how to use the platform effectively for career development.

Event Highlights

The program was led by Isha Jagat and Amarnath Sujith, who provided valuable insights and practical tips on getting the most out of LinkedIn's features. The session was designed specifically for LinkedIn newcomers and offered clear instructions on how to navigate the platform, connect with professionals, and explore job opportunities. Participants learned how to build a strong LinkedIn profile, make meaningful connections, and utilize the platform's features to further their professional goals.

Impact and Conclusion

The collaboration between FOSS and MULEARN-UCEK ensured the program was informative and engaging, providing attendees with the skills and knowledge needed to utilize LinkedIn effectively. By the end of the session, participants walked away with a comprehensive understanding of LinkedIn's potential and were equipped with the confidence to use the platform to expand their networks and achieve their professional objectives.

The program was a resounding success, with participants gaining a deeper understanding of the benefits and features of LinkedIn. The event demonstrated the value of collaboration between FOSS-UCEK and MULEARN-UCEK, and highlighted the importance of providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers. Overall, the program was a valuable resource for newcomers to LinkedIn, and provided a solid foundation for participants to build on as they continue to develop their professional online presence.
